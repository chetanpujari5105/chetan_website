About

From Curiosity to 400K Students — Here's My Story

I never planned to become an educator. I was just a curious kid from Pune who loved figuring out how things work — apps, websites, designs, AI tools. I graduated from Sinhgad College of Engineering (SIT Lonavala), but the real education happened outside the classroom.

In 2020, I uploaded my first course on Udemy. I had zero audience, zero marketing budget, and honestly — zero expectations. But something clicked. Students started showing up. Then more. Then thousands. Today, over 400,000 students from 190+ countries have taken my courses across platforms like Udemy, Skillshare, TutorialsPoint, Starweaver, Packt, Coursera, O'Reilly, and more.

But teaching online wasn't enough. I wanted to see the spark in person. So I started conducting AI workshops and guest lectures at some of India's top engineering colleges — including NIT Surat (SVNIT), where I led a hands-on Prompt Engineering workshop during their MINDBEND tech fest that had students reverse-engineering AI outputs like pros.

My teaching philosophy? I call it the Roots-to-Leaves method. Master the foundations first, and everything advanced grows naturally. I use educational psychology to structure every lesson, so even complex topics like AI agents, prompt engineering, and UI/UX design feel like a conversation — not a lecture.

I believe world-class skills shouldn't cost a fortune. That's why I make ₹80,000-worth of knowledge available for the price of a chai. ☕
